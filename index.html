Meet Your Coach

Make Room For A Clearer Choice

Ever felt stuck between different paths, unsure which decision is the right one? I know that feeling well.

I am an MSc graduate in Behavioural Economics and a certified educator with years of experience with young people. Throughout the last 15 years, while navigating major life transitions myself through endless research, reflection, and trial and error, I realised how many of us make life-changing decisions without the guidance we need.

That inspired me to found Decision Compass in June 2026, where I now work as a Youth Transition & Decision Coach. My vision is to help young people find clarity, confidence, and direction sooner, with less stress and worries by making important life decisions feel less overwhelming and more aligned with who they are and who they want to become.
